The Monash Tech Bootcamp


The Monash Tech Bootcamp is a 7-week training program designed to help
students build well rounded technology services.


We put you on a project team with other students who
come from a different specialisation.


You'll be given well-rounded business, user experience and
web development training to improve your projects


You'll be educated and support by industry
partners who are adept in their craft.

Humans of MTB
